A task focused on escalating privileges and rooting the machine.

   - The next step involved exploiting a cron job running on the machine. I found a script `.desktop.sh` in the `bootup23` user's home directory, which I modified to get a reverse shell.

   ```bash

   ```

   - I set up a listener on my machine using `nc`:
